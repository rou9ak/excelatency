## 📐 What is this?

A **living, breathing archive** of question papers, problem sets, and their solutions — spanning the intersection of quantitative rigour, abstract mathematics, and computational thinking.

Think of it as a personal exam vault, a study companion, and a portfolio of analytical depth — all rolled into one repo.

---

## 🗂️ Scope & Coverage

This repository is structured as a **multi-domain knowledge library**, covering:

### 📊 Quantitative Economics
- Microeconomic theory & game theory
- Macroeconomic modelling
- Econometrics & time-series analysis
- Mathematical economics (fixed point theorems, optimisation, etc.)

### 🧮 Mathematical Analysis
- Real & complex analysis
- Measure theory & topology
- Linear algebra & abstract algebra
- Differential equations (ODEs & PDEs)
- Probability theory & stochastic processes

### 📉 Statistics
- Frequentist & Bayesian inference
- Hypothesis testing, estimation theory
- Multivariate & non-parametric statistics
- Statistical learning theory

### 💻 Computer Science & Data Science
- Data structures & algorithms
- Machine learning & deep learning
- Mathematics for CS (discrete math, graph theory, information theory)
- Coding challenges & competitive programming
- Data science workflows, tools & theory

## 🧾 Solution Formats

Solutions appear in one of two forms:

| Format | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| ✍️ **Handwritten** | Scanned or photographed worked solutions — raw, authentic, and real |
| 📄 **LaTeX** | Typeset solutions, clean and publication-grade |

> *Both formats are intentional. The handwritten ones show the scratch-work; the LaTeX ones show the structure. Learning lives in both.*
